Email Intelligence System: Automated Email Organization and Daily Inbox Summaries

The Email Organizer and Summarizer is an Email Intelligence system designed to save businesses 1 to 3 hours and over $1000 per month. The system automatically organizes emails received over a 24 hour period into designated folders and categories by querying a database to retrieve relevant context such as email history and thread continuations. Daily inbox management includes a short 2 to 3 minute overview highlighting significant emails and actions, plus a comprehensive 5 to 10 minute breakdown document featuring inbox overview, inbox analytics, category breakdown ranked by importance, direct navigation links, and an optimized action plan. Businesses provide their desired folders and categories ordered from most to least important. Available add-ons include automated response drafts with contextual awareness, automated responses for specific email categories, text notifications for high importance emails, calendar integration for automated planning and task creation, and integration with additional automation systems.
